Hollow Knight is a 2017 2D indie platforming game designed by Team Cherry, a small Australia based team of developers. Hollow Knight has the player play as a small bug who must explore
deep into the earth and fight countless enemies to find riches, seek glory and uncover the secrets of the subterranean kingdom of Hallownest to discover its own hidden past.

"The deeper I went into Hollow Knight, the more I was surprised at just how much content and freedom it has to offer."

Publishing info:

  • Developed by: Team Cherry
  • Released: Feb 24, 2017
